<h3> Linear Regression </h3>

<p>Four linear regression models that explore the relationship between natural gas closing price and temperature,<p> 
<p>storage and residential consumption, temperature and residential consumption, and storage and temperature.<p>
<p>Also includes, a visual representation of the out of sample linear regression model for temperature and residential consumption.<p>
<p>The out of sample analysis takes temperature data for Pittsburg from 2015 through 2019 and compares actual consumption with predicted consumption.<p>
